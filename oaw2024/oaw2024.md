### Danish Open Access Week 2024 - Oct 21-25

<img src="/oaw2024/images/OAWeek2024.jpg" alt="Open Access banner" style="height: 100%; width:100%;"/>

#### [Go directly to the programme](#programme-of-the-danish-open-access-week-2024) - [Practical information](#practical-information)

##### For the third year in a row, the Danish Open Access Network is very proud to invite everyone to the Danish Open Access Week 2024. Below you can read more about the [programme](#programme-of-the-danish-open-access-week-2023) and sign up for the individual webinars.

<p>Once again, the Danish Open Access Week 2024 will include a series of different webinars on Open Access with speakers from both Denmark and abroad. Various Open Access topics will be addressed: There will be presentations that explain the basics of Open Access and presentations that will look at the more complicated Open Access aspects.</p>
<p>The webinars are open to anyone with an interest in Open Access. Below you can read the week’s programme and register for each webinar.</p>

#### Practical information
<p>The webinars are held in the course of week 43 (October 21-25 2024). For details about the individual webinars, see below. All the webinars are free of charge and anyone can sign up. All webinars are held in English.</p><p>For the sake of the climate and administration, we have decided not to record the webinars, but the slides will be made available on the site after the webinars have taken place.</p>
<p>Please note, when you have completed a registration to a webinar, you will not receive a confirmation by e-mail. The Zoom link to the webinar will appear in the message shown after you have registered for the webinar, and will be sent to you by e-mail <ins>one week</ins> and again one day before the webinar begins.</p>
